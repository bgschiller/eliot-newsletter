<Article title="Thank the Seabeck Staff" attribution="Eliot Board">

Look for the baskets on the dining hall tables starting at lunch on Sunday. This is your chance to leave a gratuity for the hardworking dining, housekeeping, and grounds staff, who've been serving us throughout camp. You can leave cash or checks (made out to Seabeck Conference Center, with "staff gratuity" on the memo line) in the baskets. If you prefer to use a credit card, you can do that in the Seabeck Office. The baskets also contain cards for you to submit comments to Seabeck (not Eliot) regarding meals and lodging.
</Article>
